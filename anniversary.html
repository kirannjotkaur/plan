From My Heart

A Letter to You

My Love,

Four years. I still don't have the right words — and maybe that's the point. You are the feeling I reach for when language fails me.

I have loved you through distance that felt like grief, through silences that tested us, through the ordinary Tuesdays and the extraordinary nights under skies we shared from miles apart. And every single time, loving you was the easiest thing I've ever done.

I think about the way you laugh — how it seems to catch you off guard sometimes, like joy surprised you. I think about your hands, the way they feel like safety. I think about all the versions of us we've already been, and I fall in love with each one again.

You are not just my love. You are my favorite thought at the end of a hard day. You are the reason I believe that some things in this world are worth every ache, every waiting room, every night I had to love you from a distance.

I don't know what the future holds. But I know I want to walk into it holding your hand. I want to grow old beside you, argue about small things, apologize with coffee, and fall asleep grateful that the universe, in all its chaos, decided to aim you at me.

Forever isn't a promise I make lightly. I make it because with you — forever still feels too short.

Always yours ♡
